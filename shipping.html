Shipping Information

Last Updated: February 2025

1. Delivery Areas

We currently offer shipping to the following regions:

  • All major cities in Morocco
  • Selected international destinations

For specific delivery availability, please enter your location at checkout.

2. Shipping Methods & Times

We offer the following shipping options:

  • Standard Delivery (2-4 business days)
  • Express Delivery (1-2 business days)
  • Same-Day Delivery (select cities only)

3. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on:

  • Delivery location
  • Package weight and size
  • Chosen shipping method

Free shipping is available for orders over 500 MAD within Morocco.

4. Order Tracking

After your order ships, you will receive:

  • A shipping confirmation email
  • A tracking number
  • Regular status updates

5. Delivery Issues

In case of delivery issues:

  • Contact our customer service within 48 hours
  • Provide your order number and tracking information
  • We will investigate and resolve the issue promptly

6. Returns & Exchanges

For returns and exchanges:

  • Items must be unopened and in original packaging
  • Return request must be made within 14 days
  • Return shipping costs may apply
